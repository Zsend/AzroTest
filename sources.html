Sources and method

Truth before marketing.

Every public number should say what it measures, what it does not measure, and when it was reviewed.

Current cannabis-incarceration count

There is no reliable, current national count covering federal, state, local, tribal, jail, prison, pretrial, and supervision systems. The widely repeated 40,000 figure is not a current verified count.

Last Prisoner Project — Cannabis Prisoner Scale

Employment outcomes

There is no reliable national employment rate specific to people with cannabis convictions. Prohibition Careers will publish only verified network outcomes.

Employer review

Criminal-history review must follow applicable law and actual role requirements. Employers remain responsible for their own legal compliance.

EEOC enforcement guidance

Publication rule

We will not count a pledge as a job, training as a career, or a start date as a retained outcome. Better data will replace older estimates when it becomes available.
