Definitions

  • BTC‑eq: total Bitcoin-equivalent accumulated through the approach over the test window.
  • Multiple: BTC‑eq vs the auto-buy baseline (1.00×).
  • Extra sats: additional satoshis accumulated vs baseline.
  • BTC accretion: annualized accumulation delta vs baseline (illustrative lens).

Important limitations

  • Backtests are not guarantees and may not reflect real execution conditions.
  • BTC is volatile and may experience deep multi-year drawdowns.
  • Taxes, custody choices, and operational constraints can materially change outcomes.
  • Use the policy + reporting templates to manage governance, not to chase historical curves.

How to use this responsibly

Treat the performance materials as a decision aid for whether a rules-based approach fits your business risk profile and time horizon. The program’s core value is discipline + governance + reporting.
