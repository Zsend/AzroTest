Pass 4 advisor review

The free floor must not depend forever on begging.

The incarcerated-user layer must remain free. The sustainable model is to fund the free floor through agencies, vendors, implementation support, certification, training, research/evaluation, philanthropy for expansion, and eventually endowment-backed reserve capital.

Review the standardReview advisor questions

Economic doctrine

Free to the user. Paid for by the system around the user.

A Right For All should not monetize prisoners or families for baseline access. The institution must earn support from the systems that benefit from a credible, measurable, implementation-ready free floor.

Institutional funding

Agency contracts, implementation support, training, technical assistance, and evaluation should fund operational work.

Standard revenue

Certification, audit, vendor integration, and procurement support can fund the standard without charging users.

Reserve capital

Endowment and reserve capital protect the mission through political, donor, and budget cycles.
