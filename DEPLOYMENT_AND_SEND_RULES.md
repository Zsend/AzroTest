# Deployment and Send Rules

## Allowed

- Private founder review.
- Counsel review.
- Advisor review.
- Password-protected partner staging.
- EVIDENCE/Dan follow-up only after they request more.

## Not allowed yet

- Public launch.
- Indexed public website.
- Medical, health, or safety claims.
- “Safe vape,” “safest,” “zero contaminants,” “certified,” “approved,” or “Proof Verified” as active.
- Cannabis sales or plant-touching operations.
- Hardware mechanism disclosure before IP review.
- Competitor accusations.
- Sending historical source PDFs to partners.

## Recommended deployment

Use password protection and keep `robots.txt`, `X-Robots-Tag`, and `<meta name="robots">` intact.
