Customer standard

Every strong claim should answer one question: what supports it?

The standard is simple. Product copy should help customers understand the workflow and the public evidence without overstating certainty, personalization, or future results.

  • Product claimsdescribe what the tool does, where it runs, and what the customer receives.
  • Scenario claimsidentify historical assumptions, limitations, and the difference between analysis and future outcomes.
  • Benchmark claimsstate the comparison, baseline, and scope.
  • Advice boundarykeeps AZRO educational and workflow-based, not personalized financial, tax, legal, custody, or security advice.

Customer boundary

AZRO helps you structure the decision. You make the decision.

Customers remain responsible for execution, position sizing, custody, exchange selection, tax treatment, and personal risk management.
