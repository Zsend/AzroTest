Product standards

Useful proof. Clear language. No hype.

AZRO is built for customers who want serious market tools, not vague performance promises. Product changes are versioned, scenarios are labeled, and public claims stay tied to what the evidence supports.

How to read AZRO material

Every strong claim should answer one question: what supports it?

The standard is simple. Product copy should help customers understand the workflow and its evidence without exaggerating certainty, personalization, or future outcomes.

  1. Product claimsdescribe what the tool does and where it runs.
  2. Scenario claimsidentify historical assumptions and limitations.
  3. Benchmark claimsstate the comparison, baseline, and scope.
  4. Review claimsshould only use words like reviewed or audited when that exact review exists.
