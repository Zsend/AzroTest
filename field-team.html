Program structure

What every field report should answer.

Require practical evidence rather than vague endorsements. This protects the brand and creates conversion assets that customers believe.

Runtime proof

  • Start and end battery percentage
  • Actual Starlink Mini usage hours
  • Weather and temperature notes

Setup proof

  • Time to deploy
  • Where cables confused users
  • What needs labels or color coding

Sales proof

  • Who asked about buying
  • What price felt acceptable
  • What objection came up first
