Reserve Standard — Homepage cinematic scroll system (V15)

What changed
- Added true section-to-section transition chapters between major homepage sections.
- Each transition is generated from the real chapter metadata already in the homepage.
- Transition scenes use a sticky full-screen dark stage, animated seam line, motion beam, oldstyle editorial numerals, and chapter handoff copy.
- Motion is tied to actual scroll progress, not a generic hover or simple fade.
- Reduced-motion users fall back to a stable, non-animated version.
- Mobile and tablet layouts collapse into a simpler, safer presentation instead of forcing the desktop effect.

Design intent
- Make scrolling feel like moving through chapters of a premium treasury memo.
- Add visible motion-graphic quality without turning the site into a gimmick.
- Preserve the approved typography, spacing system, and document-like authority.
