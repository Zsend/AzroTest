What this bundle contains
- index.html ................. full product microsite
- capture-gallery.html ....... screenshot-ready gallery with live slide previews
- gallery.html ............... alias of the capture gallery
- slide-01-cover.html ........ fixed 1920×1080 cover artboard
- slide-02-process.html ...... fixed 1920×1080 process artboard
- slide-03-modes.html ........ fixed 1920×1080 modes artboard
- slide-04-accountability.html fixed 1920×1080 accountability artboard
- slide-05-access.html ....... fixed 1920×1080 access artboard
- slide-06-fit.html .......... fixed 1920×1080 audience-fit artboard
- slides.css ................. shared slide design system
- site.css ................... microsite stylesheet (imports slides.css)
- fit.js ..................... scales artboards and live previews to the browser window
- assets/screens/ ............ local dashboard screenshots used in the slides
- assets/previews/ ........... preview PNGs for gallery fallback

Best workflow for Gumroad
1. Open capture-gallery.html.
2. Open the slide you want.
3. Screenshot the full 16:9 artboard.
4. Upload those captures to Gumroad.

Source of truth
- The live slide pages are the source of truth.
- The preview PNGs are included only as gallery fallbacks.
