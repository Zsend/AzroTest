# Source notes for factual accuracy

The redesign copy is based on the current PowerStream website pages reviewed on June 2, 2026. The goal was to modernize presentation without inventing capabilities or guarantees.

## Core company positioning

Source: `https://www.powerstream.com/`

Used facts:
- PowerStream is a Lund Instrument Engineering, Inc. company.
- PowerStream describes itself as a product-development resource with industrial design, power supplies, battery chargers, DC/DC converters, batteries, and plastic injection molding.
- The company specializes in custom, semi-custom, and off-the-shelf power supplies, battery chargers, UPS, and battery backups.
- PowerStream says it engineers and manufactures products in Utah and China.
- Address and phone: 1163 South 1680 West, Orem, Utah 84058, USA; 801-764-9060; fax 801-764-9061.

## About / company history

Source: `https://www.powerstream.com/power3.html`

Used facts:
- Founded in Utah in 1992.
- Purchased by Lund Instrument Engineering, Inc. in January 2000.
- Moved into a 16,000-square-foot Orem facility in October 2009.
- Installed injection molding presses and an injection-mold machine shop in 2011.
- Works in power supplies, battery chargers, batteries, UPS technologies, industrial design, sourcing, and manufacturing.

## Intentional exclusions

The prototype does not claim:
- ISO certification.
- UL/CE approvals across all products.
- Exact live inventory.
- Exact pricing.
- Customer logos or named endorsements.
- Guaranteed delivery dates.
- A functioning backend contact submission endpoint.
- A replacement for product-specific datasheets, policy documents, or ecommerce checkout.
