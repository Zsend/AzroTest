Questions that usually decide the sale.

If these answers make sense to you, AZRO is probably a fit.

Is this day-trading software?

No. AZRO is built for weekly and cycle-level decisions, not intraday action.

Why not just auto-buy BTC?

Auto-buy is a solid default. AZRO is for people who want a written weekly process that adapts to cycle context and keeps score against that baseline.

Why buy XRP if BTC is the foundation?

Because XRP can have the bigger cycle. The indicator exists to help serious users handle that opportunity with more discipline instead of guesswork.

Does it repaint?

Confirmed weekly-close outputs are the stable record. EARLY can change before the weekly close by design.

Do I need paid TradingView?

Basic use can work on free plans. Higher alert capacity and webhook features depend on your TradingView tier.
