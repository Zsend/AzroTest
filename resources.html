FAQ (short)

Fast answers. For full detail, open the FAQ PDF.

Is this a day‑trading tool?

No. Confirmations evaluate on the weekly close. It’s designed for cycle‑level decisions.

Does it guarantee tops/bottoms?

No. It’s an indicator, not certainty. Treat outputs as one input into a risk‑managed plan.

Can I use it on other assets?

The model is tuned for XRP cycle behavior. v1.0.9 sunsets “WARNING” labels on non‑XRP charts. Non‑XRP risk triangles can be enabled, but do not assume cross‑asset reliability.

How do I get access after buying?

Purchase on Gumroad, then provide your TradingView username. Access is granted after verification. If you forgot to include your username, email support with your receipt.
