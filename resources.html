Quick answers.

The basics people usually want clarified before they commit.

Is this day-trading software?

No. AZRO is built for weekly and cycle-level decisions.

Does it guarantee tops or bottoms?

No. Use the outputs inside a written plan. Markets can change and models can fail.

Does it repaint?

Confirmed weekly-close outputs are the stable record. EARLY can change before the weekly close by design.

How do I get access?

Purchase or start a trial, add your TradingView username at checkout, then access is granted through TradingView Invite.

Why should most people start with BTC?

Because BTC is the foundation and the destination. The BTC Operating System is the clearest first step for calmer long-term Bitcoin building.

Why is XRP involved at all?

Because XRP can have the bigger cycle. The XRP indicator exists to help serious users handle that opportunity with more discipline.
