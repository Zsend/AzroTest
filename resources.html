Quick answers.

The direct answers to the questions serious buyers usually ask first.

Is this a day-trading tool?

No. It is designed for weekly, cycle-level context.

Does it guarantee tops or bottoms?

No. Treat labels and alerts as inputs inside a written risk process. Markets can change and models can fail.

Do I need a paid TradingView plan?

Basic use can work on lower tiers. Webhook alerts and higher alert capacity depend on your TradingView plan.

How do I get access after purchase?

Purchase on Gumroad, add your TradingView username at checkout, and invite-only access is granted after verification.

Why should most people start with BTC?

Because BTC is the destination. The BTC Operating System is the clearest starting point for calm, rules-first long-term Bitcoin building.

Why does XRP exist in a Bitcoin-first brand?

XRP is the smaller cycle tool. It exists to help around major cycle turns and, when used well, to strengthen the long-term BTC plan.
