Our public standard

AZRO makes claims only inside their evidence boundary.

Model changes are versioned. Public claims are controlled. Historical scenarios are labeled. Performance-style statements require assumptions and evidence. Educational content is not personalized financial, tax, legal, custody, or security advice.

Model change logs

Material model, docs, benchmark, data-basis, and release changes are logged by version. Output-affecting changes require a new release record.

Claim gates

Benchmark, performance, proof, review, and superiority language must match its evidence class. Stronger wording requires stronger support.

Scenario limits

Backtests and historical scenarios are presented under stated assumptions and are not promises of future results.

Educational boundary

AZRO is a workflow and education system, not individualized advice.

AZRO does not provide individualized financial, investment, tax, legal, custody, brokerage, exchange execution, or security advice. Users remain responsible for their own decisions, execution, custody, tax treatment, and risk management.

How this works with Proof Shield

Proof Shield controls evidence integrity. Brand Trust Standards control public wording.

Proof Shield is designed to make backdating, cherry-picking, and silent revision materially harder. Brand Trust Standards are designed to keep public claims disciplined so the company does not imply more than the evidence supports.
