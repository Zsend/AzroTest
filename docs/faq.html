What does the indicator do?

It highlights statistically significant cycle extremes on XRP using a multi‑layer, weekly‑evaluated composite. The goal is to provide clearer “major inflection” context—not intraday entries.

Which timeframe should I use?

1‑Week only. Confirmations are evaluated on the weekly close. Lower timeframes are unsupported.

Do signals repaint?

Confirmations do not repaint: MAJOR / RADAR labels finalize at the weekly close.

EARLY heads‑ups can change: EARLY labels are intra‑week staging signals and may disappear if the week fails to confirm.

Which exchanges or pairs are supported?

Any TradingView symbol that tracks XRPUSD (spot or perpetual). For consistency, we recommend BITSTAMP:XRPUSD for long history.

Do I need a paid TradingView plan?

No. The script can run on the free tier. The main difference is how many alerts you can run and how long alerts remain active. Paid tiers can make “set‑and‑forget” alerting easier.

How do I set alerts correctly?

Always set alerts on the 1‑Week chart. In the alert dialog, choose a named condition (e.g., MAJOR TOP) rather than “Any alert() function call”.

What’s the difference between MAJOR and RADAR?

Both are weekly‑close confirmations. MAJOR is the primary confirmation track; RADAR is a complementary track that can provide an alternative confirmation timing.

Can I automate trades from alerts?

TradingView alerts can be routed to third‑party automation bridges via webhooks. If you choose to automate, test with small size and understand execution, slippage, and outage risk.

What are WARNING / RISK labels on non‑XRP charts?

They are optional market‑wide risk context derived from XRP conditions. They are meant to help you manage exposure and risk—not to function as buy/sell confirmations for that altcoin.

Is access lifetime?

Yes—purchase is a one‑time payment for lifetime invite‑only access tied to one TradingView username, plus future updates for licensed users.

Can I share the script or use it on multiple TradingView usernames?

No. Licenses are single‑user and non‑transferable. Sharing, reselling, or redistribution violates the Terms and can result in access revocation.

Refunds?

If you have unresolved technical issues, contact support within 14 days of purchase for help and potential refund resolution. Access is revoked upon refund.
