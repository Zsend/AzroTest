2. How We Use Data

Fulfill orders and grant TradingView access. Send product updates, educational content, and occasional promotions (opt-out anytime). Improve performance and security of our services.

3. Cookies & Analytics

We use privacy-focused analytics (Plausible) and session cookies for login persistence. Plausible is cookieless and stores no personal data.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We never sell your data. Third-party processors: Gumroad (payments), TradingView (script delivery), Plausible (analytics).

5. Data Security

AWS S3 encrypted storage; access controlled under the principle of least privilege.

6. Retention

  • Email: Only retained if you subscribe; removed immediately upon unsubscribing.
  • TradingView Username: Retained permanently to ensure uninterrupted lifetime access to the indicator (contract necessity).
  • Transaction Data: Retained for 5 years (tax compliance).

7. Your Rights

Access, correction, deletion, portability, and objection. Email support@azrosystems.com.

8. Children

Services are not directed to individuals under 18.

9. Changes

We will email registered users and post a notice of material updates. Educational only. Trading involves risk; hypothetical results have limitations. See Master Disclaimer & Notices.
