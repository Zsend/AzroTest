# Fair Chance Employer Compact

## Purpose

The compact converts broad social-impact language into a measurable hiring operating system. Signing is an application, not certification.

## Required commitments

1. **No blanket conviction ban.** Exclusions must be tied to a legal requirement, license, actual duties, timing, and individualized review.
2. **Skills-first process.** Detailed conviction history is not exposed in the matching marketplace. Employers follow applicable pre-offer and post-offer rules.
3. **Transparent compensation.** Every role has a genuine range, employment type, schedule, location, benefits, and manager.
4. **No second-chance discount.** No reduced wage because a candidate is justice-impacted, no candidate fee, no unpaid trial shift, and no coercive probationary arrangement.
5. **Privacy and dignity.** No forced public story, mugshot, marketing participation, or broad internal disclosure.
6. **Individualized review.** Where history may legally matter, the employer considers the nature and gravity of conduct, time elapsed, duties, rehabilitation, accuracy, and candidate context with counsel-approved process.
7. **Advancement plan.** Each role identifies training, promotion, supervisory, licensing, profit-sharing, or ownership pathways.
8. **Outcome reporting.** Employers report interview, offer, start, pay, benefits, 30/90/180/365-day status, advancement, complaint, and separation reason.
9. **Candidate correction rights.** Candidates may challenge inaccurate background or licensing information and provide context.
10. **Nonretaliation.** No retaliation for exercising privacy, correction, fair-chance, wage, safety, organizing, or accommodation rights.

## Membership levels

### Pledged

- Signed compact
- Named executive sponsor
- Stated quarterly role commitment
- No public implication that hiring outcomes have occurred

### Verified

- Policy and application review complete
- Hiring-manager training complete
- At least one approved role
- Compensation and background process reviewed
- Candidate escalation contact assigned

### Proven

- Confirmed career starts
- Required outcome reporting current
- Candidate safety/trust checks completed
- Retention and advancement evidence
- No unresolved material complaint

## Enforcement

Status may be paused, suspended, or revoked for inaccurate claims, discrimination, privacy breach, wage theft, coercion, unsafe work, non-reporting, retaliation, licensing misrepresentation, or failure to honor a role.

## Employer value proposition

Justice Grows supplies:

- Candidate sourcing and skills normalization
- Role and jurisdiction pathway review
- Fair-chance workflow templates
- Federal Bonding Program coordination where applicable
- Reentry, document, transportation, training, and retention partners
- Candidate-consented introductions
- 30/90/180-day support
- Auditable impact reporting
- Public recognition tied to evidence

## Founding cohort offer

Recruit 25–50 employers across regulated, ancillary, remote, hemp, training, and relocation pathways. Require a minimum role commitment; waive initial platform fees; fund candidate support through employer sponsorship and philanthropy. Do not charge candidates.
