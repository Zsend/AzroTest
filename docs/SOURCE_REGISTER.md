# Official Source Register

Last operational review: June 18, 2026. This register is a starting point, not legal advice. Jurisdiction rules require effective-date tracking and counsel review.

| Domain | Official source | Operational use | Review note |
|---|---|---|---|
| California fair-chance hiring | California Civil Rights Department — Fair Chance Act | Employer workflow, timing, individualized review | CRD states the law generally covers employers with five or more employees and prohibits asking about conviction history before a job offer, among other requirements. |
| Federal employment discrimination | U.S. EEOC — arrest and conviction record guidance | Narrowly tailored policy, individualized assessment, confidentiality | Use with current case law and counsel; do not convert guidance into an automatic rule engine. |
| Employment background reports | Federal Trade Commission — Background Checks: What Employers Need to Know | FCRA boundary, disclosure/authorization, pre-adverse/adverse action | Justice Grows should not act as an employment consumer-reporting agency or adverse-screening product without a separate compliance model. |
| Federal Bonding | U.S. Department of Labor — Federal Bonding Program | Employer risk-reduction referral | DOL describes no-cost fidelity bonds covering the first six months for qualifying hires. Confirm worker, role, and state process. |
| Work Opportunity Tax Credit | Internal Revenue Service — WOTC | Potential incentive only after current authority is confirmed | The official page reviewed June 18, 2026 states authorization for workers beginning by December 31, 2025. Do not market WOTC as currently available without an extension and tax advice. |
| California corrections records | California Department of Corrections and Rehabilitation — Media Policies/Public Records | Releasable identity, commitment, sentencing, release-action fields; records requests | CDCR lists name, commitment offense, CDCR number, facility assignment, sentencing, and release actions among information that may be released, subject to limits. |
| California cannabis rules | California Department of Cannabis Control — DCC Regulations | Role/license/badge pathway review | Distinguish license-applicant/owner rules from employee badge rules; check current regulations and local overlays. |
| Occupational taxonomy | U.S. Department of Labor O*NET Web Services | Normalize candidate skills and job duties | Production integration should use a server-side API adapter, preserve candidate language, and record O*NET version. |
| Federal custody status | Federal Bureau of Prisons Inmate Locator and FOIA | Known-identifier custody/release monitoring and recurring extracts | Locator status is not a complete judgment or sentence record; “released” may be release from BOP custody, not freedom from all custody/supervision. |
