Quick Start Guide

Step-by-step beginner guide for installing the indicator, setting alerts, and using the XRP Top/Bottom Detector safely.

Back to Resources Download PDF

Last updated: 2025-12-23 · Educational only. Not investment advice.

Important: Always run and set alerts on the 1‑Week chart. Lower timeframes are not valid for this indicator.

Who it’s for

  • New or learning traders who want a simple, high‑conviction framework.
  • Seasoned swing‑traders and long‑term investors looking to time multi‑year cycle extremes.

What you can do with it

  • Time the XRP cycle with clarity — posts major buy/sell zones on XRP, highlights mid‑cycle turns, and confirms MAJOR/RADAR at the weekly close.
  • Accumulate near cycle bottoms (MAJOR bottoms) and scale out near cycle tops (MAJOR/RADAR tops).
  • Use as a directional filter for any lower‑timeframe system you already trade (trend alignment).
  • Not designed for intraday or scalp signals.
  • LIGHT cues (XRP) provide earlier-in-the-turn timing heads‑ups at the weekly close (optional).
  • Risk context — optional RISK/HIGH‑RISK triangles visualize rising pressure (XRP) and can be enabled for non‑XRP charts.
  • Alt‑coin risk layer — non‑XRP charts can show market‑wide WARNINGs when composite conditions deteriorate.

Easiest & most common playbook

Buy EARLY BOTTOM → Sell MAJOR TOP or RADAR TOP

  1. Accumulate on EARLY BOTTOM heads‑ups (or wait for MAJOR/RADAR BOTTOM at the weekly close if you prefer confirmation).
  2. Hold through the trend.
  3. Exit on the first weekly TOP confirmation that prints — MAJOR TOP or RADAR TOP.
  4. RADAR note: RADAR TOP can be the exact top; keep both TOP alerts on. RADAR BOTTOM (optional) can help surface developing bottoms.
  5. Optional tools (advanced): LIGHT TOP/BOTTOM and RISK/HIGH‑RISK triangles can refine timing and risk, but are not required to call tops or bottoms.

What prints and where (scope check)

This is the fastest way to avoid misconfigured alerts: XRP charts use TOP/BOTTOM confirmations (MAJOR/RADAR) and optional LIGHT/RISK cues. Non‑XRP charts show WARNING labels and optional RISK triangles (no tops/bottoms).

  • EARLY WARNING (non‑XRP) — alert‑only; fires intra‑bar when composite conditions deteriorate.
  • MAJOR WARNING (non‑XRP) — weekly‑close label/alert once risk extremes confirm; historically marks the start of deeper alt‑coin drawdowns.
  • RADAR WARNING (optional, non‑XRP) — weekly‑close market‑wide warning tied to XRP’s RADAR conditions.
  • RISK / HIGH RISK triangles (optional, non‑XRP) — weekly‑close visual caution phases (modes: Ultra / Tight / Adaptive / Standard).
  • XRP exclusivity — Buy/Sell confirmations remain XRP‑only. Non‑XRP charts show WARNINGs and optional RISK triangles only.

Beginner safety brief (short)

  • Decide risk, size, and exits before clicking Buy. If a loss would disrupt your life, risk is too high.
  • Cycle tools are designed to reduce screen‑time. The goal is fewer, higher‑quality decisions.
  • Avoid “get rich quick” thinking. Focus on education, patience, and repeatable process.
  • If trading makes you anxious or hopeless, close the platform and seek support.
