Data & goal

The industry can count jobs. It still cannot count who gets shut out.

We will not invent precision. We will show what is known, what is missing, and what Prohibition Careers will measure.

Scale, not vacancies

A large employment base exists. Access is the missing system.

The figures below are not directly comparable. One is an industry employment base. One is an incomplete historical prison estimate. Together, they show scale—not available openings or guaranteed capacity.

Legal cannabis employment baseEarly 2024 estimate
≈440K
Historical rough cannabis-prison estimateNot current or complete
≈32K

Read this correctly: roughly 440,000 positions were supported across the legal industry; they were not 440,000 open jobs. The ≈32,000 figure is a historical extrapolation, not a current verified count.
