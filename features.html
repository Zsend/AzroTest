Key Features

Buy & Sell labels

Clear labels at likely cycle turning points help you accumulate near bottoms and take profit near tops. Turn on alerts so you never miss them.

Heads‑up alerts

Optional early alerts nudge you before a turn may complete—useful for planning entries and exits without watching the screen.

Risk Warning (XRP + all crypto)

When market risk rises, the indicator prints a Risk Warning on your XRP chart and across other crypto charts—use it to tighten risk, reduce size, or step aside.

Top Warning (market‑wide)

When the broader market overheats, you’ll see a Top Warning across crypto. On XRP it coincides with a Sell label; on alt‑coins it prints as a market‑wide warning—handy to de‑risk before weakness or before collapse.
