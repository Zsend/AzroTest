Design principles.

These are the constraints that keep the product premium, useful, and safer than a random parts bundle.

1

No inverter tax.

Use DC-native architecture wherever possible instead of paying for oversized AC output that the main use case does not need.

2

Fuse every branch.

Customer-ready means the kit removes wiring ambiguity: clear ports, fused paths, protected harnesses, and polarity-controlled setup.

3

Modular first.

Start with a Core DC Kit, then let users add battery, solar, and vehicle charging without replacing the whole system.
