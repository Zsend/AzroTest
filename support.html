FAQ.

Use this as the customer-ready baseline. Update the answers after prototype testing and supplier validation.

Is CragLink affiliated with Starlink?

No. Starlink Mini and Starlink are trademarks of SpaceX. CragLink Power is intended as a third-party compatible power accessory/system and is not affiliated with or endorsed by SpaceX or Starlink.

Will it run Starlink Mini all day?

The Basecamp Box is designed around a 100Ah LiFePO4 architecture, which is the right class of battery for all-day Starlink Mini use. Final runtime will depend on the confirmed hardware, temperature, Starlink draw, cable length, solar conditions, and accessory loads.

Why not just buy a power station?

Generic power stations are convenient, but they often include high-wattage AC inverters and features that add cost and losses for a low-power DC Starlink Mini use case. CragLink is designed to be more purpose-built.

Can it charge from a vehicle?

The product ladder is designed to support a vehicle-charge path. Final vehicle charging specs should be validated with the manufacturer before launch.

Is this for climbers only?

No. The brand is climber-tested because climbers are demanding gear users, but the system is for anyone who wants reliable basecamp internet power: outdoor enthusiasts, overlanders, guides, creators, remote workers, and car campers.

Retailer, gym, guide, or team?

Use the founder form to express dealer or group-buy interest. This is especially useful for gyms, teams, guide services, camps, and outdoor retailers.
