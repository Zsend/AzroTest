FAQ

Fast answers.

These keep the site useful without turning it into a full e-commerce store.

Can I buy online?

No checkout is included in this first version. Call or visit the store for current availability and purchases.

Does inventory change?

Yes. Live animal and feeder inventory can change quickly, so the site avoids stale product listings.

Where should I check new arrivals?

Follow @mark_ark_slc on Instagram for visual updates, then call the store if you want to confirm availability.

Should I call before visiting?

Yes, especially if you need a specific species, feeder size, tank, bulb, food, or specialty supply.

What should I ask before buying a reptile?

Ask about adult size, enclosure size, diet, temperature, humidity, lighting, handling, and lifespan.

What city is Mark’s Ark in?

The store is in Taylorsville, Utah, in the Salt Lake City metro area.
