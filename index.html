Setup details

Fast answers to the most common setup questions.

What timeframe is supported?Weekly (1W) chart only.

Weekly (1W) only. The detector is designed for cycle-level confirmation on XRP using weekly data. Lower timeframes are unsupported.

Does it repaint?Confirmed labels evaluate on the weekly close.

Confirmed labels evaluate on the weekly close. Earlier/context labels can appear during the week and may change before the weekly bar closes. For decision-grade confirmations, rely on the weekly-close labels and alerts.

How should I set TradingView alerts?Confirmed vs early/context triggers.

Most users set confirmed alerts to Once per bar close (weekly) and any early/context alerts to Once per bar.
