How it works

Four steps. Zero drama.

Make it boring. Make it durable. Make it repeat.

1) Set a cash buffer target

Protect payroll and near-term obligations first. BTC is the reserve — not the operating account.

2) Choose a weekly contribution

A fixed dollar amount or % of surplus. Start small; consistency beats intensity.

3) Select an execution mode

Auto-buy, accumulate-only, or “valve smooth” (disciplined pacing). No discretionary sells.

4) Review monthly reporting

Know your BTC balance, BTC‑eq, cash runway, and policy compliance at a glance.

Execution modes (plain English)
Mode Intent When to use Complexity
Auto‑buy Simple weekly DCA. Predictable, easy, and emotionally neutral. Default for most teams Low
Accumulate‑only Only buys when conditions meet the policy. Skips weeks that don’t. When you want rules, not timing Medium
Valve Smooth Disciplined pacing that can concentrate buys into favorable windows while staying policy‑bound. For the most disciplined operators Medium+

All modes are designed to avoid leverage and discretionary trading. The point is a repeatable treasury habit.
