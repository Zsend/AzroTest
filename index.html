What it does

A weekly, cycle‑aware indicator built to reduce emotional decision‑making. It’s not a promise — it’s a structured way to interpret extremes.

Cycle confirmations

MAJOR TOP/BOTTOM prints only when multi‑layer consensus and timing alignment are present.

Parallel confirmation track

RADAR provides an alternate confirmation path to capture late‑phase structures (bottom path optional).

Context without noise

EARLY marks stage during the week, LIGHT cues provide structure‑aware heads‑ups, and RISK overlays highlight caution windows.

Designed for weekly decisions
Confirmations evaluate on the 1W bar close. Moves between weekly closes are not confirmations. If you trade lower timeframes, treat this as a high‑level cycle layer — not a scalping tool.
