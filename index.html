Not a big-box pet aisle

Part pet shop. Part field trip.

Mark’s Ark should feel like the place you stop by just to see what came in: tanks, terrariums, aquatics, reptiles, feeders, supplies, and staff who can point you in the right direction.

This first version keeps the brand personality from Instagram — live animals, quick updates, local energy, and a little edge — while making the business easier to find on Google.
