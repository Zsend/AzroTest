Why this matters now

You did not build a strong business just to let its cash quietly lose strength.

This is not only about investment return. It is about whether the money your business fought to earn will still carry real strength into the future.

The squeeze is already here. The things businesses actually buy often rise faster than the official story suggests. The dollar keeps crumbling while payroll, taxes, insurance, software, inventory, and financing keep compounding.

Technology should be lowering costs. Weak money absorbs that benefit and forces owners to run harder just to hold ground. Doing nothing is not neutral. Doing nothing is already a treasury decision.
