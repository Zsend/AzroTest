Treasury logic
Not every dollar should do the same job.
Most businesses blur the buckets — operating cash, safety cash, opportunity cash, and long-run treasury capital become one pile. The pile quietly weakens.
Operating cash

Payroll, vendors, taxes, software, inventory, and business continuity.

Safety cash

Time, resilience, and room to absorb shocks without panic.

Opportunity cash

Dry powder for obvious high-return moves inside the business.

Bitcoin treasury capital

Long-term stored business energy carried forward in a harder, digitally native reserve asset.
