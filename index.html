FAQ

Clear answers before families apply.

The best puppy families appreciate honesty. These answers reduce repeat questions and prevent overpromising.

Are puppies available now?

No. This is a planned litter page and early interest list. Breeding and pregnancy must be confirmed before puppies are promised.

Can we meet the parents?

Yes. Approved families can request a parent meet-up or call through the application. This is one of the easiest ways to build trust before reserving.

Do you take deposits yet?

No. Applications are free. Deposits should only happen after litter status is confirmed and written price, deposit, refund/transfer, and health terms are shared.

Are doodles hypoallergenic or non-shedding?

No breeder can honestly guarantee that every doodle will be hypoallergenic or non-shedding. Coat, shedding, and allergy response can vary by puppy and by household.

What will puppies go home with?

The final go-home package will be confirmed before deposits open. The intended plan includes veterinary records, age-appropriate care details, transition guidance, and ongoing support.

Where are you located?

Doodles in Utah is based in Riverton, Utah, serving families across the Wasatch Front and nearby Utah communities.

How do you choose homes?

We look for families who are prepared for training, grooming, veterinary care, time commitment, and the real responsibility of raising a puppy.
