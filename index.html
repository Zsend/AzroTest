The gap

A record can become a ceiling long after the sentence ends.

Cannabis moved from contraband to commerce. Companies, careers, and wealth followed. Many people who carried the consequences did not.

The problem is not that standards exist. The problem is when one old fact becomes the entire evaluation.

A conviction should not earn someone a job. It should not erase their ability—or decide how far they can go.
