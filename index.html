The gap in cannabis vapor trust

Most vapor products rely on oil COAs and vendor claims. Proof Labs focuses on the full system: what the oil touched, what heat changed, and what came out under defined conditions.

Material path

What did the oil touch?

Reservoirs, seals, metals, ceramics, adhesives, polymers, mouthpieces, heaters, airflow paths, and unknowns must be mapped before the system can be understood.

Heat behavior

What did heat change?

Thermal behavior, hotspots, chain-pulls, near-empty operation, and heater aging can change the system profile beyond the original oil COA.

Aerosol output

What came out?

The relevant proof question is not only what went into the device. It is what comes out after storage, use, and defined stress conditions.
