The standard

Not charity. Better hiring.

Nobody is hired because of a conviction. Nobody should be rejected before their ability is seen.

For candidates

Bring evidence of who you are now.

A clear goal, honest communication, reliable follow-through, skill or serious learning, and lawful fit for the opportunity.

For employers

Review the actual person for the actual role.

Keep legitimate standards. Identify real legal restrictions. Consider current evidence before making the decision.

The scale

The industry can count jobs. It still cannot count who gets shut out.

That missing information is not a reason to guess. It is a reason to build a transparent connection and measurement system.

Legal cannabis employment base≈440K

Full-time-equivalent jobs reported in early 2024. This is not a vacancy count.

Historical rough prison estimate≈32K

Not current, complete, or confirmed as wholly nonviolent.

Cannabis-conviction employment rateUnknown

No reliable national measure currently exists.

The point is not to compare unlike numbers.

The point is scale: a large legal employment base exists, while access and outcomes for people carrying cannabis convictions remain largely invisible.
