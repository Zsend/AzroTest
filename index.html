Principles →

Essentials inflate faster

Many operators feel purchasing power loss more acutely than headline CPI because housing, healthcare, childcare, education, and insurance absorb a larger share of real budgets.

“Good jobs” tread water

Nominal raises can look fine while real purchasing power stays flat. That pushes more pressure onto side income, ownership, and systems that scale.

AI squeezes the middle

Task automation compresses admin/ops work while rewarding owners who build distribution, process leverage, and durable assets.
