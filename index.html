A Right for All

Empowering Inmates Through Education

About Us

A Right for All is a nonprofit organization dedicated to raising awareness about the systemic barriers to education within the U.S. criminal justice system.

Our Mission

We believe open-source education, like Khan Academy, should be accessible to everyone—especially incarcerated individuals. In the modern world, this open-source resource is freely available, yet it remains out of reach for prisoners.

Why It Matters

As taxpayers, we bear the cost each time individuals are incarcerated again. Ensuring access to education can help break this cycle, providing inmates with the skills they need to build better futures and reduce recidivism.
