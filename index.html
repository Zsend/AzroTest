Power that moves with you

Engineered for basecamps. Built for anything beyond.

Generic power stations are designed to run everything. CragLink is designed around one valuable job: keep Starlink Mini powered efficiently at camp, then recover from solar or your vehicle when the trip keeps going.

Support

Questions before launch.

Does CragLink include Starlink Mini?

No. CragLink is designed as a compatible power system concept for Starlink Mini users. It does not include Starlink hardware or service.

Is this a final shipping product?

Not yet. The site is structured for founder demand, prototype validation, and partner conversations. Final specs, certifications, pricing, and warranty terms should be confirmed before taking payment.

Why not just use a power station?

You can. The CragLink thesis is that Starlink Mini is a low-power DC load, so a purpose-built DC system can be simpler, more efficient, and more field-specific than a generic AC power station.

Can it charge from a vehicle?

The recommended architecture is vehicle-charge-ready through a proper DC-to-DC charging module, not a raw cable from a car outlet to a lithium battery.

Is this waterproof?

The target should be weather-resistant field use, not submersion. Final ingress protection depends on enclosure, connectors, panel, and electronics selected for production.
