What AZRO Systems is

A simple program with conservative defaults: convert surplus into BTC reserves while protecting operations and staying audit-ready.

Policy-first treasury OS

Written guardrails define what happens weekly and monthly — so execution is repeatable and reviewable.

Operations protected

A dedicated cash buffer reduces the risk of forced selling. Liquidity comes first; BTC allocation comes from surplus.

Board-approvable artifacts

Templates and reporting that finance teams can actually use: policy memo, roles/controls, KPI report, exception log.

How it works (60 seconds)
1

Set a buffer policy

Define a conservative cash buffer floor that protects payroll and obligations (operations first).

2

Allocate a weekly budget

Choose an amount you can sustain through drawdowns. Consistency matters more than size.

3

Execute with rules

No prediction. No discretionary trading. Follow a mode that is explainable, repeatable, and documented.

4

Report monthly

Track BTC, BTC-eq, buffer coverage, baseline comparison, fees, confirmations, and exceptions.
