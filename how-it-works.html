How it works

A fair chance needs a real process.

Prohibition Careers is a private connection layer—not a public job board. We prepare the person, verify the opportunity, make the introduction, and track what happens next.

01

Prepare

Build a clear record of who someone is now.

Current work. Relevant skills. References. Goals. Readiness. Lawful fit.

Not a rewritten criminal record. A professional record of present ability.

02

Connect

Reach a real decision-maker.

A specific person meets a real role, paid project, apprenticeship, or business mentor.

No public candidate database. No vague hiring pledge.

03

Prove

Let the work create the next signal.

An interview, bounded paid project, apprenticeship, or job gives both sides a responsible way to evaluate fit.

04

Grow

Measure more than a start date.

Retention, pay, responsibility, promotion, leadership, procurement, and ownership show whether opportunity became mobility.

Candidate readiness

Ready for the opportunity.

  • Clear directionA realistic role or field.
  • Current evidenceWork, projects, references, credentials, or a responsible way to test ability.
  • Honest communicationClear facts and known constraints.
  • Reliable follow-throughResponsiveness, preparation, and consistency.
  • Lawful fitThe role, jurisdiction, and individual circumstances align.

Employer readiness

Real work. Clear terms.

  • Paid opportunityNo productive unpaid tests or candidate-paid programs.
  • Defined workRole, scope, success criteria, and decision-maker.
  • Clear compensationWage, project fee, hours, benefits, and timing.
  • Real restrictionsLaw, licensing, safety, and role requirements—not assumptions.
  • Room to growPerformance can lead to more responsibility.
