Compatibility and launch notes.

Clear boundaries before launch protect customer trust, product safety, and partner conversations.

Compatibility

CragLink Power is a concept-stage power system intended for Starlink Mini users. It does not include Starlink hardware, Starlink service, or official Starlink affiliation.

Prototype status

All specifications, pricing, runtime claims, certifications, warranty terms, and component selections must be verified through engineering review, supplier quotes, and field testing before taking payment or shipping production units.

Safety

Lithium battery systems require proper fusing, charge control, wiring, thermal protection, transport compliance, and user instructions. Final production should be reviewed by qualified electrical and compliance professionals.

Brand usage

Use “compatible with Starlink Mini” descriptively unless a formal partnership or authorization exists.
