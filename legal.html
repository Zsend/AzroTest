Compatibility, safety,
and support.

BaseRelay is in validation. This page explains the current compatibility posture, safety boundaries, warranty status, and what is not being sold yet.

Compatibility

BaseRelay is designed for Starlink Mini compatible remote-basecamp use. BaseRelay is independent and is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by Starlink or SpaceX.

Safety posture

The first BaseRelay path is validation-first. Custom battery systems, battery inventory, solar charging, and vehicle charging are deferred until architecture, protection, support, warranty, and validation gates are clear.

Warranty status

No public hardware warranty is offered through this site because no public hardware sale is active. Warranty terms will be published before any paid batch or hardware shipment.

Orders, payments, and inventory

This site does not accept hardware orders, paid reservations, pre-launch orders, or inventory commitments. The validation list is for updates, tester interest, and future controlled pilot invitations only.

Use limitations

Do not rely on BaseRelay materials as final electrical, safety, transportation, legal, warranty, or emergency-use guidance until final product documentation is published for a specific configuration.
