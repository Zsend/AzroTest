Compatibility principles
DC-native

Designed around clean DC output and avoiding unnecessary inverter loss.

Fused

Every production harness should use appropriate fusing, labeling, and polarity control.

Solar

Solar input targets should be validated against panel voltage, controller limits, heat, shade, and real sun.

Vehicle

Vehicle charging should use a proper DC-DC charging path, not direct unsafe alternator-to-lithium wiring.

Warranty

Warranty terms should follow final production sourcing.

This site is founder-stage marketing. Final warranty, shipping, returns, certifications, and support commitments should be published only after PowerStream or another manufacturing partner confirms the production BOM and compliance path.

◇Prototype claims

Founder-stage performance targets should be labeled as targets until field validated.

⌘Safety documentation

Final kits should ship with fuse ratings, wiring diagrams, polarity notes, and operating limits.

◎Support scope

Define who handles warranty, troubleshooting, returns, and compatibility questions before taking paid orders.

▣Partner approvals

Use “compatible with Starlink Mini” carefully unless an official partnership exists.

Shipping and returns
Battery rules

Lithium battery shipping requirements depend on final battery configuration, carrier, packaging, and jurisdiction.

Return condition

Set clear return rules for used batteries, cables, solar panels, and field-damaged equipment.

Lead times

Founder drops should state estimated ship windows and clearly separate deposits from final purchase orders.

Regional support

Start with one launch region before expanding internationally.
