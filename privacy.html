Privacy

Private by default.

Professional ability should become visible without turning conviction history into a public profile.

Launch-stage data practice

This website uses no analytics, advertising trackers, third-party fonts, account creation, file uploads, or public candidate database.

The intake tool prepares an email in your browser. Information is not sent to Prohibition Careers until you choose to send that email.

Do not send sensitive records

Do not send Social Security numbers, dates of birth, court files, identification documents, medical information, immigration records, background reports, or complete criminal histories through the public website or ordinary email.

Sharing

Candidate information should not be shared with a prospective employer or service partner without the candidate’s knowledge and an identified purpose. Personal stories, photographs, and conviction details are not published without specific informed permission.
