Privacy

Your story belongs to you.

The pilot is designed to collect less, keep information private, and never require public storytelling.

Effective June 22, 2026No candidate feesNo public profiles

What this website collects.

At launch, Prohibition Careers does not use analytics, advertising pixels, tracking cookies, user accounts, or a candidate database.

The connection forms prepare an email on your device. Your answers remain in your browser until you choose to open your email application or copy and send the message yourself.

What we receive.

When you email us, we receive the information you place in that email, along with ordinary email metadata such as the sender address, date, time, and routing information.

We ask for basic contact, work, opportunity, and partnership information. Do not send Social Security numbers, dates of birth, identification documents, court files, medical information, immigration documents, background reports, or detailed criminal histories through the website or an unsolicited email.

How information may be used.

  • to review a request, opportunity, or proposed connection;
  • to communicate with you;
  • to evaluate possible fit between a person and an opportunity;
  • to improve the network and prevent misuse;
  • to comply with law or protect people and the service.

When information may be shared.

Candidate information is not placed in a public profile. We do not sell personal information. We will seek the candidate’s permission before sharing identifiable candidate information with a prospective employer or service partner, except where disclosure is required by law or needed to prevent serious misuse or harm.

No participant is required to publish a conviction, photograph, testimonial, or personal story to receive consideration.

Retention and deletion.

We intend to keep information only as long as it remains reasonably useful for the pilot, a requested connection, recordkeeping, security, or legal obligations. You may ask us to delete your email and related working records. Some copies may remain temporarily in backups or where retention is legally required.

Hosting and third parties.

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may process ordinary server and security logs under its own policies. Email is hosted through Purelymail. Links to other organizations lead to their own services and privacy practices.

Adults and cannabis work.

This site is intended for adults. Cannabis-industry roles may require a minimum age, residency, licensing, background review, or other qualifications that differ by jurisdiction and role.

Contact.

Privacy questions and deletion requests: support@prohibitioncareers.com.
