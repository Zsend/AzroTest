Privacy

Respectful by default.
Simple by design.

BaseRelay only asks for enough information to communicate about validation, product updates, and controlled pilot opportunities.

Information collected

If you join the validation list, BaseRelay may collect your email address and any information you choose to provide about your remote-power use case.

How it is used

Information is used to send product updates, evaluate tester fit, improve the validation program, and communicate about future controlled pilot opportunities.

Sharing

BaseRelay does not sell personal information. Information may be processed by form, hosting, email, analytics, or CRM providers used to operate the site and validation list.

Your choices

You can unsubscribe from email updates or request deletion of your validation-list information by contacting BaseRelay through the contact method published with the final domain.
