Privacy overview

This website uses a simple application message flow. When you submit or copy the application, the information you provide may be sent by email, text, or Instagram direct message depending on how you choose to contact us.

What we collect

Application details may include your name, email, phone number, city/state, home situation, dog experience, puppy preferences, and questions.

How it is used

Information is used to respond to your inquiry, understand whether the timing and puppy fit may work, schedule a parent meet-up if requested, and manage the interest list.

No sale of information

Application information should not be sold. It should only be used for puppy-list communication and related follow-up.

Contact

Questions can be sent to hello@doodlesinutah.com.
