About AZRO Systems

Two systems. One rules-first mindset.

AZRO Systems builds proof-first TradingView workflows that reduce noise, support a written plan, and keep weekly decisions calm enough to follow.

BTC Operating System turns Bitcoin volatility into a repeatable allocation process with one clear action, one amount, and proof versus auto-buy. XRP Top/Bottom Detector turns cycle conditions into readable labels, optional context, and alerts you can review on a disciplined weekly-close routine.

BTC glossary (with short videos)

What the dashboard means, and how it fits into a repeatable Bitcoin allocation process.
Action + suggested amountOne clear weekly output: BUY / TRIM (optional) / HOLD plus a matching amount.

Read the dashboard after the weekly close and execute the action that matches your written policy.

Market contextHigh-level cycle orientation designed for decisions, not noise.

Use the context layer to understand whether the system is operating in accumulation, neutral, or elevated-risk conditions.

Modes + risk profilesChoose the policy that matches your philosophy, risk tolerance, and time horizon.

Stack, Adaptive, Event, and Performance change how the operating process responds across the cycle. Conservative, Balanced, and Aggressive tune the posture.

Proof vs auto-buyBTC-eq, extra sats, and accretion keep the process accountable.

The proof layer shows whether the process is adding BTC-equivalent versus a simple auto-buy baseline, directly on chart.

Alerts (optional)Weekly reminders plus rare high-attention prompts.

Alerts are there to support the routine, not to turn the process into constant chart-watching.

XRP label glossary (with short videos)

What each label means, and how it fits into a weekly-close workflow.
MAJOR TOP / MAJOR BOTTOMCycle-level confirmations designed to anchor the biggest decisions.

Treat these as decision points — not predictions. Use them to follow your written exposure and exit rules.

RADAR TOP / RADAR BOTTOMA secondary confirmation track that complements MAJOR labels.

Still weekly-close based. Use it to add structure, not urgency.

LIGHT TOP / LIGHT BOTTOMHeads-up labels for timing and awareness.

Designed to be useful without being noisy. Validate against your plan and risk limits.

EARLY TOP / EARLY BOTTOMIntra-week staging cues that can appear and disappear.

Helpful during the week, but only confirmations persist at the weekly close.

Risk triangles + context overlaysOptional layers that add regime context without clutter.

Use them to tighten rules, reduce exposure, or avoid emotional decision-making — not as entries.
