Label glossary

Short definitions of what you’ll see on the chart. Everything is designed around the weekly close. “EARLY” labels are intra‑week staging cues and can disappear.

MAJOR TOP / MAJOR BOTTOM

Cycle‑level confirmations on the weekly close. Low frequency by design. Treat these as decision points—not predictions.

Weekly close confirmation

RADAR TOP / RADAR BOTTOM

A secondary confirmation track that complements MAJOR signals. Still weekly‑close based—use it to add structure, not urgency.

Weekly close confirmation

LIGHT TOP / LIGHT BOTTOM

Heads‑up labels for timing and awareness. Designed to be useful without being noisy. Not a guarantee; validate against your plan.

Heads‑up

EARLY TOP / EARLY BOTTOM

Intra‑week staging cues. Helpful for context during the week, but only confirmations persist at the weekly close.

Can appear intra‑week

RISK / HIGH‑RISK triangles

Risk context markers. Use them to tighten rules, reduce exposure, or avoid emotional decision‑making—not as entries.

Context overlay

Market‑context overlays (optional)

Optional non‑XRP risk triangles and related context controls to help you assess broader crypto regime risk (informational).

Optional

Alert setup (one‑time)

Recommended defaults: set MAJOR and RADAR alerts to Once per bar close (weekly). Set EARLY alerts to Once per bar if you want intra‑week heads‑ups. More detail in the Alert Trigger Guide.

The goal is fewer, higher-quality decisions — and less emotional trading.
