Label glossary (with short videos)

Everything is designed around the weekly close. “EARLY” labels can appear intra-week as staging cues and may disappear before the weekly close.

Core confirmations

MAJOR TOP / MAJOR BOTTOM Cycle-level confirmations (weekly close). Low frequency by design.

Treat these as decision points—not predictions. Use them to follow your written exposure and exit rules.

RADAR TOP / RADAR BOTTOM A secondary confirmation track that complements MAJOR labels.

Still weekly-close based. Use it to add structure, not urgency.

Optional context layers

LIGHT TOP / LIGHT BOTTOM Heads-up labels for timing and awareness (context, not certainty).

Designed to be useful without being noisy. Validate against your plan and risk limits.

EARLY TOP / EARLY BOTTOM Intra-week staging cues (can appear and disappear).

Helpful during the week, but only confirmations persist at the weekly close.

RISK / HIGH-RISK triangles Risk context markers (informational overlays).

Use them to tighten rules, reduce exposure, or avoid emotional decision-making—not as entries.

Market-context overlays Optional broader-crypto regime context (informational).

Optional non-XRP risk triangles and related context controls to help assess broader crypto regime risk.

Alert setup (one-time)

Recommended defaults: set MAJOR and RADAR alerts to Once per bar close (weekly). Set EARLY alerts to Once per bar for intra-week visibility. More detail in the Alert Trigger Guide.
