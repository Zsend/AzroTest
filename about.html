Label meanings, setup notes, and walkthrough clips for the weekly XRP confirmation workflow.

MAJOR TOP / MAJOR BOTTOMCycle-level confirmations designed to anchor the biggest decisions.

These are the highest-priority confirmation labels in the XRP workflow. Use them as written decision points on the weekly close.

RADAR TOP / RADAR BOTTOMA secondary confirmation track that complements MAJOR labels.

RADAR remains weekly-close based. Use it to add structure to top-side and optional bottom-side decisions without forcing noise.

LIGHT TOP / LIGHT BOTTOMHeads-up labels for timing and awareness.

LIGHT labels are there to help you see local exhaustion or base-building without replacing the confirmation stack.

EARLY TOP / EARLY BOTTOMIntra-week staging cues that can appear and disappear before the weekly close.

EARLY is the faster heads-up layer. Useful if you deliberately want earlier information, but not required for the base workflow.

Risk triangles + context overlaysOptional layers that add regime context without clutter.

Use RISK / HIGH RISK and the optional context layers to tighten rules, reduce exposure, or frame the broader regime — not as a substitute for MAJOR / RADAR confirmations.
