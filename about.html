About AZRO Systems

Two products. One rules-first mindset.

AZRO Systems builds proof-first TradingView tools for a calmer weekly process: BTC Engine for dashboard-led Bitcoin allocation, and the XRP Top/Bottom Detector for cycle confirmation with clean on-chart labels.

Built for clarity, durability, and conservative execution.

Both products are designed to reduce noise, support a written plan, and keep decisions anchored to a weekly process instead of emotional chart-watching.

BTC Engine

A weekly Bitcoin operating system.

BTC Engine is built for long-term allocators who want a calmer routine: one weekly action, one suggested amount, and proof-first tracking against a simple auto-buy baseline.

  • Weekly action + suggested amount
  • Modes + risk profiles for different operating styles
  • Cash-buffer-aware sizing
  • Built-in proof vs auto-buy (BTC-eq, extra sats, accretion)

BTC Engine glossary

The dashboard is designed to answer the most important weekly question first: what action does the engine suggest, and why?

Weekly action + amountThe engine suggests BUY, HOLD, or TRIM and a matching weekly amount.

Use this as a workflow anchor for the week. It turns the dashboard into an execution routine instead of a constant monitoring habit.

Market stateHigh-level context such as accumulation, neutral, or risk.

Market state helps frame the environment without pretending to predict every move. It is there to support weekly decisions, not intraday reaction.

Modes + risk profilesDifferent ways to run the engine, from straightforward stacking to more adaptive behavior.

Modes define how optional trims are used. Profiles shape how much cash the engine tends to preserve versus deploy.

Proof vs auto-buyBTC-eq, extra sats, and accretion compare the engine to a simple baseline.

These are proof metrics, not promises. They keep the process honest by showing whether the workflow is actually adding value versus a simpler default.

Cash bufferSizing is built around a dedicated cash reserve.

The engine is designed to respect a cash buffer first, then become more aggressive only in stronger value conditions according to the selected mode and profile.

Label glossary (XRP)

The XRP Detector is built around a weekly-close workflow. EARLY labels can appear during the week; MAJOR and RADAR confirmations are designed to matter at the weekly close.

Core confirmations

MAJOR TOP / MAJOR BOTTOMCycle-level confirmations designed to anchor major decisions.

Use MAJOR labels as decision points — not predictions. They’re built for the moments when you want your plan to be clearest.

RADAR TOP / RADAR BOTTOMA secondary confirmation track that complements MAJOR labels.

RADAR adds structure around the bigger turns. It’s there to sharpen a weekly process, not create urgency.

LIGHT TOP / LIGHT BOTTOMHeads-up labels for timing and awareness.

LIGHT labels are optional context. Leave them on if you want more texture between major turns; turn them off if you prefer a cleaner confirmation-only chart.

EARLY TOP / EARLY BOTTOMIntra-week staging cues that can appear and disappear.

EARLY is there for awareness during the week. Use it as context, not as a replacement for weekly-close confirmations.

Risk triangles + context overlaysOptional layers that add regime context.

Risk triangles, price coloring, and context overlays are there to frame the environment without turning the chart into noise.
