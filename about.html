Label glossary

The indicator’s labels are designed to communicate signal strength and timing context. Use alerts on the labels you care about, and ignore the rest.

Radar Top / Radar Bottom

Early warnings that the cycle may be approaching an inflection zone. Best used as a “pay attention” alert, not a standalone entry/exit.

Major Top / Major Bottom

Higher-conviction labels intended to confirm a more meaningful cycle pivot. Often paired with a risk-managed plan (scale-outs / scale-ins).

Final Warning

A late-cycle caution signal. Consider tightening risk rules, reviewing exposure, and preparing an execution plan.

Extreme Low

A deep-cycle condition that may appear near capitulation zones. Designed for investors who plan accumulation with strict sizing and time horizon discipline.

Dashboard glossary

BTC Engine is designed to be read in under a minute, once per week. Here’s how to interpret the core dashboard fields.

Action

The engine’s suggested weekly action: BUY, TRIM, or HOLD. It’s a context-aware recommendation, not a guarantee.

Mode

Execution philosophy (stack, adaptive, event, performance). Modes change how aggressively the engine deploys weekly budget across cycle conditions.

Profile

Risk posture (conservative, balanced, aggressive). Profiles shift the engine’s sensitivity to cycle signals and its sizing behavior.

BTC-eq

A performance lens that compares your engine-driven execution against a simple auto-buy baseline over the same period.
