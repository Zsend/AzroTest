Public accountability

Evidence, matching, and impact methodology

Authority comes from showing what is known, how it was established, what remains uncertain, and how quickly errors are corrected.

Operating draft · Counsel review required before collecting real sensitive data
Publication sentence

“No violent offense was identified in the current commitment records reviewed as of the stated date.” The platform does not define a human being by a permanent label.

Unit of analysis

The registry evaluates the current correctional commitment or supervision basis. It does not infer eligibility from an arrest label, press article, old conviction, or incomplete docket. Cannabis-only and cannabis-primary cases remain distinct.

Minimum publication gate

  1. Resolve identity across source systems.
  2. Confirm current custody or supervision status.
  3. Establish cannabis attribution to the current commitment.
  4. Review all material current counts, enhancements, consecutive terms, detainers, and resentencing orders.
  5. Apply and date the violence-screen statement.
  6. Document source provenance and last-check time.
  7. Obtain two independent approvals with every checklist item complete.

Confidence states

Confirmed

Operative court or commitment records support the material fields.

Supported

Multiple reliable records align, but a primary record remains outstanding.

Provisional

Private research only. Never included in confirmed public totals.

Disputed

Held or annotated while conflicting evidence or a correction is resolved.

Freshness and coverage

Every public record carries a last verification date. Projected release dates are labeled separately from confirmed release events. Coverage is measured by jurisdiction, source availability, acquisition cadence, and operational review capacity. Missing data is unavailable—not zero.

Career matching

Matching uses candidate-selected skills, target roles, location, remote and relocation preferences, lawful pathway, schedule, and compensation. Detailed conviction information is intentionally excluded from automated scoring. Licensing and role eligibility receive separate human and legal review before an introduction.

Impact metrics

Targets never appear as results. A job start is counted only after confirmation. Retention is reported by explicit intervals. Wage, promotion, credential, ownership, and entrepreneurship outcomes require dated evidence. Small cohorts are suppressed when disclosure could identify a participant.

Correction protocol

Credible disputes involving identity, custody, violence screening, release, or safety trigger field freezing or temporary unpublishing, preservation of the prior value, independent re-review, a dated change note, and notice to the affected person when contact information exists.
