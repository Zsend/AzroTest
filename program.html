Step-by-step

The goal is to participate in long-run monetary repricing while protecting the business. That means the program is designed to survive volatility, not pretend it doesn’t exist.

1) Set the treasury boundary

Determine a weekly contribution amount (or a fixed % of cash flow) that is sustainable through cycles. Start small (e.g., $20/week) and increase only if it remains operationally safe.

Rule: the business comes first.

2) Establish a buffer

Hold a dedicated cash buffer so the business is never forced to sell BTC to meet near-term obligations. This is a core control that prevents “bad sells” during drawdowns.

Buffer reduces forced timing risk.

3) Run a rules-based accumulation mode

Weekly buys are executed according to predefined rules (not discretion). The “Valve” modes in the packet illustrate how allocation intensity can change across regimes.

Discipline > prediction.

4) Optional trims for liquidity & optionality

The program can include an optional trim policy: systematic reductions during overheated periods to rebuild buffer, fund growth, or reduce concentration—without “calling the top.”

The business can harvest optionality without becoming a trading desk.

5) Monthly reporting

A treasury program is only as good as its visibility. Use the monthly report template to track BTC position, cost basis, allocations, and policy compliance.

What gets measured gets managed.

6) Periodic review & governance

Update rules only with deliberate review—not reactively. Governance is part of the product: written memo, decision log, and auditable process.

Stability is a feature.

Who it’s for

Good fit

  • Owners with consistent surplus cash flow after essentials, taxes, and buffers.
  • Teams that want written policy and accountability, not discretionary “trading.”
  • Operators who can hold through volatility and think in multi-year horizons.

Not a fit

  • If your business needs the cash in the next 6–18 months.
  • If a 50–80% drawdown would force operational decisions.
  • If you want leverage or frequent tactical trading.

Important

AZRO materials are educational. Before implementation, align with your tax and legal advisors and confirm how you will custody assets (self-custody vs institutional solutions).
