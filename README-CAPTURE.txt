What this bundle adds:

- Dedicated slide pages are included for screenshot capture:
  slide-01-cover.html
  slide-02-process.html
  slide-03-modes.html
  slide-04-accountability.html

Recommended workflow:
1) Open capture-gallery.html.
2) Use the 1920×1080 links when your display is large enough and you want the highest-quality capture.
3) Use the 1600×900 links when you want the easiest manual screenshot workflow on a smaller display.
4) Go full-screen and screenshot the entire visible artboard.
5) Upload the PNG to Gumroad.

Notes:
- The slide pages are built on a 16:9 artboard with safe margins for screenshot capture.
- Exo 2 is loaded from Google Fonts when available.
- The main landing page is still available in index.html.
