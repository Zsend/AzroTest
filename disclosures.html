Disclosures

This site and the attached materials are for informational purposes only. Please read carefully.

Not investment advice

AZRO provides educational materials, templates, and a rules-based framework. We are not your financial advisor, and nothing here should be interpreted as individualized investment advice, a solicitation, or a recommendation.

Bitcoin risk

Bitcoin is volatile. Prices can decline significantly and for extended periods. You can lose some or all of the money you allocate. Only allocate what you can afford to hold through volatility.

Simulated / backtested performance

Any performance figures based on simulations or backtests are hypothetical and have limitations. They do not represent actual trading and may not reflect the impact of market liquidity, slippage, outages, behavioral deviations, and many real-world constraints. Past performance is not indicative of future results.

Tax and accounting

Tax treatment varies by jurisdiction and entity type. Consult qualified professionals regarding tax, accounting, and reporting requirements for your business before implementing a treasury program.

Custody and operational security

Digital asset custody involves operational risk, including key management and counterparty risk. Use appropriate controls, consider multi-signature arrangements where appropriate, and document procedures.
