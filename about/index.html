Independent public-interest initiative

Built in public. Clear about what it is.

A Right For All is an independent, founder-led initiative developing evidence, standards, a movement, and practical paths toward a free education and basic-wellness baseline on correctional tablets.

Current promise

Human potential is not disposable.

The first demand is free GED prep and basic wellness on every correctional tablet.

Current status

A Right For All is not currently presented as a nonprofit, tax-exempt organization, government program, approved pilot, vendor product, or official facility program.

No donation claims

The site does not accept donations or describe contributions as tax-deductible. A future entity and funding structure may be developed later.

No implied endorsement

References to agencies, facilities, vendors, educators, researchers, or public bodies do not imply partnership or endorsement unless explicitly stated.

Letters and provenance

Evidence with a human source.

Each published letter was personally given to the founder by its writer for use in A Right For All. Typed excerpts are lightly edited for readability and remain paired with the original documents.

Corrections and removal

A writer may request a correction, updated attribution, or removal. The founder’s legal name is removed from the founder’s public letter. One additional writer is anonymized for safety while in custody; unredacted sources remain preserved privately.

Request a correction or removal

Narrow privacy review

Home addresses, phone numbers, case identifiers, names of minors, sensitive third-party details, and information creating a specific safety concern may be withheld or redacted.

Privacy and participation

The site collects as little as possible.

Contact form

The help form opens the visitor’s own email app. The site does not automatically collect or store the form contents.

Challenge

Challenge answers save only in the visitor’s browser until exported. The static site does not transmit them.

No advertising or behavioral tracking

The current site includes no advertising, behavioral analytics, or third-party application backend.

No employment promise

Contact or Challenge participation does not promise selection, paid work, employment, partnership, funding, or a response.

Important boundary

Information and advocacy—not medical, legal, or crisis care.

A Right For All does not provide medical diagnosis, therapy, crisis response, legal representation, or correctional-facility services.

In the United States, people experiencing suicidal thoughts or an immediate mental-health crisis can call or text 988. In an emergency, contact local emergency services.

Public contact

Questions, professional input, Challenge packets, evidence corrections, and removal requests:

hello.arightforall@gmail.com

Keep the first message concise. Do not send confidential legal documents, unnecessary medical information, or sensitive information about another person without first confirming an appropriate review path.
