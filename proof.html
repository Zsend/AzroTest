What each level means

Clear words. Clear evidence.

Proof levels describe a participating employer’s highest completed, documented stage. They do not certify every past or future employment decision.

Level 1

Policy verified

Compact commitments, responsible contacts, candidate privacy, story rights, and a role-specific fair-chance process were reviewed.

Level 2

Role verified

A live paid role passed employment, compensation, duties, pathway, and publication review.

Level 3

Hire verified

A candidate confirmed a career start after a candidate-controlled introduction.

Level 4

Retention verified

A qualifying start reached at least 180 days, confirmed through the outcome workflow.

Level 5

Mobility verified

A candidate confirmed a raise, credential, promotion, equity grant, or ownership pathway.
