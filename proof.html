What AZRO is proving

AZRO is not trying to prove that the future is guaranteed. AZRO is building a public operating record so people can judge the system by process, discipline, version control, limitations, and consistency over time.

A proof system should preserve the record when conditions are easy and when conditions are not. That is what makes it useful.

The public proof layer is designed to show

  • current BTC benchmark context;
  • weekly public proof entries;
  • version history;
  • revision status;
  • public-safe founder notes;
  • limitations and disclosures.

The public proof layer is not designed to reveal

  • Pine source;
  • thresholds;
  • formulas;
  • exact trigger conditions;
  • raw chart exports;
  • internal proof-facts;
  • customer or private material.
