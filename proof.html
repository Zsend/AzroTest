Benchmark standard

The benchmark is weekly auto-buy.

A business can always buy the same dollar amount of Bitcoin every week. That is the baseline. Reserve Standard only earns its place if the same dollars can build a stronger Bitcoin reserve over time through a disciplined weekly process.
