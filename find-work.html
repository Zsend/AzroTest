For people ready to move forward

Your record is not your résumé.

We help capable people with nonviolent cannabis convictions show what they can do now—and compete for work with room to grow.

Bring proof of now

You do not need a perfect story.

You need an honest way to show what you can contribute.

A job you did well. A project you finished. A skill you can demonstrate. A problem you know how to solve. A person who can speak to your work.

Work

Jobs, contracts, physical work, operations, service, or responsibility carried consistently.

Projects

Products, systems, designs, research, code, art, business attempts, or problems solved.

References

People who can speak to reliability, judgment, learning, leadership, or quality.

Growth

Training, changed habits, sustained effort, and evidence that you finish what you start.

What matters now

Be ready to earn the opportunity.

A cannabis conviction does not guarantee an introduction. Current readiness matters.

Career growth

Privacy

Your story stays yours.

No public profile. No forced personal story. No candidate fee. We ask before sharing your information with an employer.

When law blocks a role

We tell the truth and look for another path.

Direct cannabis work is not lawful for every person in every jurisdiction. Other paths may exist through cannabis-adjacent companies, professional services, technology, design, logistics, remote work, mentorship, and entrepreneurship.
