Registry concept

The future product is a machine-readable proof record.

Proof Registry is the future system of record for CVA IDs, versions, conditions, limitations, corrections, expiry, and revocation status.

Registry is memory.

A PDF report is static. A registry can store status, limits, version history, correction notices, and machine-readable proof records that operators, retailers, insurers, regulators, and autonomous systems can eventually query.
