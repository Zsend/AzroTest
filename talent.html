For talent

Your record is part of the story. It is not your résumé.

If a nonviolent cannabis conviction has blocked work that fits your ability, Prohibition Careers helps you organize the evidence of who you are now and reach real decision-makers.

Join the network

Build a New Record

Bring the evidence a background check cannot show.

You do not need a perfect résumé or a public redemption story. You need a clear direction and credible evidence of what you can contribute now.

That evidence can come from formal employment, independent projects, caregiving, coaching, technical work, creative work, business attempts, community responsibility, or difficult work completed consistently.

01Work

Jobs, projects, products, portfolios, and responsibilities completed.

02Skill

Tools, judgment, knowledge, craft, and problems you know how to solve.

03Reliability

References, consistency, preparation, and evidence that you finish what you start.

04Direction

The roles you want, why they fit, and where you are ready to begin.

Selective by design

Opportunity is not automatic. The evaluation is fuller.

Active introductions depend on fit, readiness, available opportunities, lawful eligibility, and something credible an employer can evaluate.

Clear role goal

You can describe the work you are trying to reach.

Honest communication

You can explain your present situation without hiding material facts or making the conviction your whole identity.

Evidence or learning potential

You can show what you already know—or why a responsible employer should believe you can learn quickly.

Follow-through

You respond, prepare, show up, and handle commitments seriously.

Lawful fit

The role is available under the laws, licenses, and requirements that apply.

Privacy by default

Your story belongs to you.

No public profile

Your conviction and personal information are not posted in a searchable candidate database.

No story required

Support is never conditioned on press, photographs, testimonials, or social-media content.

No sensitive records

Do not send Social Security numbers, dates of birth, court files, IDs, medical information, or background reports through the website.

More than one lane

Cannabis first. Not cannabis only.

Some people want to work directly in legal cannabis. Others are better served by cannabis-adjacent or broader fair-chance careers. The choice should follow ability, ambition, law, and fit—not a label.
