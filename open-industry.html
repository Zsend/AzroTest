When law blocks the door

Employer willingness cannot override licensing law.

Some states and roles restrict direct cannabis-industry work based on conviction history. Those limits must be identified honestly.

When direct placement is not lawful, opportunity may still exist through cannabis-adjacent technology, design, packaging, logistics, professional services, remote work, procurement, mentorship, entrepreneurship, and policy reform.
