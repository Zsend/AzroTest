Operator Brief

Bitcoin solves the asset problem. It does not automatically solve the operating problem.

Reserve Standard is built for the second problem: keeping the plan clean enough to survive real business life.

The reserve operating layer

The core question is not whether Bitcoin exists. The question is whether a business can build a long-run reserve without threatening operations, losing optionality, or turning capital allocation into another source of chaos.

Reserve Standard separates the jobs of cash, defines a written policy, installs a weekly cadence, and keeps a Reserve Record that can be reviewed over time.

The standard

  • Protect operating cash first.
  • Keep safety and opportunity cash separate.
  • Use only the long-run reserve bucket for Bitcoin accumulation.
  • Run one weekly review process.
  • Judge the outcome against the same budget baseline.
