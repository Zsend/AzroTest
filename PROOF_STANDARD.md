# Justice Grows Proof Standard

## Governing rule

**Claims are signals. Outcomes are proof.**

Public credit is earned only by completing the relevant evidence path. Every metric must identify the event, source, owner, timestamp, verification status, and deduplication rule.

## Verified zero

Zero means the active data plane contains no record that has completed the required proof path. It does not prove that no qualifying activity exists elsewhere. Missing, inaccessible, or unreviewed information is never converted into a positive or negative conclusion.

## Employer proof ladder

| Level | Public meaning | Minimum evidence |
|---|---|---|
| Applied | Private lead only | Complete employer application |
| Compact signed | Commitment recorded | Authorized signatory accepts all compact terms |
| Policy verified | Process reviewed | Identity, hiring owner, fair-chance process, privacy, story rights, and reporting reviewed |
| Role verified | Real opportunity | Open, funded, paid role with compensation, duties, manager, location, licensing path, and expiration |
| Introduction approved | Candidate agency | Candidate explicitly approves the employer introduction |
| Hire verified | Paid start | Candidate-confirmed start; employer confirmation when practical |
| Retention verified | Durable employment | Candidate-confirmed 90/180/365-day milestone |
| Mobility verified | Upward mobility | Candidate-confirmed raise, promotion, credential, equity, or ownership event |

The public employer ledger displays only verified employers that separately consent to public attribution. It shows the highest completed stage and underlying counts. Sponsorship cannot buy a badge or ranking.

## What does not count as a job or hire

- A logo, pledge, donation, sponsorship, or coalition membership
- A general careers page
- A scraped, expired, unpaid, speculative, or unverified listing
- A job without a compensation range or accountable hiring owner
- An introduction the candidate did not approve
- An offer without a paid start
- A start reported only by marketing staff
- A story, testimonial, photo, or press release

## Candidate outcome rules

- Public starts, retention, and mobility require candidate confirmation.
- Retention or advancement cannot precede a verified start.
- Separation is tracked privately and neutrally.
- Wage, benefits, schedule, safety, stability, advancement, and ownership matter; starts alone are insufficient.
- Small cohorts are suppressed to reduce re-identification risk.

## Industry-claim review

A submitted company claim is a private research lead, not an accusation or score.

A public finding requires:

1. A stable public source
2. A precise claim statement
3. Defined evidence sought
4. Documented research
5. Neutral evidence note
6. A completed right-of-reply step for material conclusions
7. Staff approval and timestamp

Possible public dispositions are:

- Verified role located
- Verified outcome located
- No qualifying evidence located

“No qualifying evidence located” means the reviewed material did not meet the standard. It does not mean the original statement was necessarily false.

## Registry publication gate

A person is never published from a tip alone. Publication requires:

- Identity resolution beyond name alone
- Current commitment and all material counts reviewed
- Cannabis attribution supported by operative records
- Current custody or release status checked
- At least two documented sources
- At least one court, commitment, or official records source
- Two distinct trained reviewers completing every required check
- Supported or confirmed confidence
- No unresolved hold or reject decision
- Dated verification and correction path

## Corrections

Credible high-risk disputes trigger an immediate field freeze or record pause. All material changes preserve the prior value, new value, reason, source, reviewer, timestamp, and public correction note where appropriate.
