# CragLink final color and conversion strategy

## Decision
Use one controlled environmental gradient across the site:

- Header / hero: Arctic Cyan + Midnight Navy = trust, satellite signal, precision, cold-weather technical credibility.
- Middle: Glacier Mint + Seafoam Sage = nature, basecamp, calm, outdoor durability without feeling soft or earthy.
- Footer / final CTA: Alpine Gold + Copper Ember = warmth, expedition, alpenglow, rugged durability, and a stronger emotional close.

## Conversion rule
Keep primary CTAs Arctic Cyan through the buying journey so the user learns one action color. Use Alpine Gold only for the final warm close and founder-reservation moment.

## Logo rule
Do not redraw the mark. The top sparkle/dots stay Platinum White. The mast is one clean fade from white to cyan. No random mixed colors at the top.

## Claims rule
Until prototypes are validated, use “design target” and “target pricing” rather than definitive performance claims.
