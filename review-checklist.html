Founder Review

Review checklist before local staging

Use this to decide whether the proof-site draft is directionally approved for local staging. It does not replace QA or launch approval.

Founder decisions

  • Approve the redaction line: public accountability, protected engine.
  • Approve or edit the page copy direction.
  • Choose placeholder-first benchmark entry or real first proof entry.
  • Defer numeric benchmark deltas unless methodology/review is fully ready.
  • Confirm BTC remains core and XRP remains optional amplifier.

Logic protection

  • No Pine source.
  • No thresholds.
  • No formulas.
  • No trigger logic.
  • No exact timing recipe.
  • No raw chart exports.
  • No proof-facts bodies.
  • No customer/private material.

Pricing / trial links

In the standalone preview, pricing/trial/support/resources links point back to this checklist. In local staging, those must be wired to the real current AZRO pages.

Resources

resources.html should remain the docs/resources hub. It should not become the proof archive.

Support

Support path must remain reachable after local staging.

Disclosures

Final staged site must preserve risk disclosure, terms, privacy, and educational/non-advice limitations.

Existing site links

Products, pricing, resources, support, risk, terms, and privacy links need to be validated against the current site base during local staging.
