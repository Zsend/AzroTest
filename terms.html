Terms

Clear standards. Honest limits.

Prohibition Careers is a pilot connector. These terms explain what the site does—and what it does not promise.

Effective June 22, 2026Pilot in formation

No guaranteed outcome.

Submitting information, joining the network, or communicating with Prohibition Careers does not guarantee a response, referral, introduction, interview, offer, employment, promotion, license, record relief, funding, or any other result.

Hiring remains with the employer.

Employers define roles, evaluate candidates, make hiring decisions, and remain responsible for applicable employment, cannabis, licensing, background-check, wage, safety, and workplace laws. Prohibition Careers does not employ candidates or make hiring decisions for employers.

Standards are not lowered.

A cannabis conviction does not qualify a person for a role. Candidates must still meet the legitimate requirements of the work and provide honest information. Employers are not asked to ignore genuine legal restrictions or material role-related risk.

No legal or professional advice.

Information on this site is general. Prohibition Careers does not provide legal, tax, immigration, licensing, financial, mental-health, or other professional advice. Laws and eligibility rules vary and can change.

Candidate use is free.

Prohibition Careers does not charge candidates to join the pilot or be considered for a connection. No employer may require a candidate to pay Prohibition Careers for access to a role.

Paid work must be real.

Employers and partners must describe compensation and material requirements honestly. Productive unpaid “tests,” multilevel marketing, candidate-paid opportunities, deceptive commission-only arrangements, and unlawful work are not accepted.

Privacy and stories.

Do not submit sensitive records through the site. Personal stories, conviction details, photographs, testimonials, and employer logos may not be published by Prohibition Careers without appropriate permission.

Public data and corrections.

Public figures are presented with sources and limits. They may be revised as better data become available. A historical estimate is not a current count, and a federal sentencing series is not a national incarceration count.

Formation status.

Prohibition Careers is a pilot initiative in formation. The site does not claim tax-exempt status, and payments are not represented as tax-deductible unless a qualified entity or fiscal sponsor is expressly identified in the future.

Contact.

Questions or reports of misuse: support@prohibitioncareers.com.
