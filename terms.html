Terms overview

This page is a plain-English overview for families interested in Sweetie and Oso’s planned doodle litter. Final purchase terms, deposit terms, health agreement, and go-home details should be provided in writing before any deposit is accepted.

Planned litter status

Breeding, pregnancy, birth, litter size, puppy availability, color, coat, size, and temperament are not guaranteed. The website may accept interest-list applications before a litter is confirmed.

Applications

Applications are free and do not guarantee approval, reservation priority, or a puppy. They are used to understand family fit, timing, and next steps.

Deposits

Deposits are not open while the litter is unconfirmed. If deposits are later accepted, the amount, refund/transfer rules, payment method, and puppy selection process should be shared in writing first.

Health and coat claims

No guarantee is made that any puppy will be hypoallergenic, non-shedding, a specific adult size, a specific color, or a perfect fit for every home. Veterinary records and final health terms should be shared before go-home.

Good-home standard

The goal is to place puppies with prepared families who understand grooming, training, veterinary care, and the long-term responsibility of dog ownership.
