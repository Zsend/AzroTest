Platform standard

Terms of participation

The platform is designed to create opportunity without overstating legal, employment, licensing, or release outcomes.

Operating draft · Counsel review required before collecting real sensitive data
No guaranteed outcome

Justice Grows does not promise release, employment, licensing, record relief, relocation, funding, or legal results. Information is not legal advice.

Candidate participation

Candidates must provide information in good faith and control their own visibility. Participation is free. Candidates may decline any role, introduction, service, media request, or public profile.

Employer participation

Employers must provide real, currently funded roles; transparent compensation; a documented fair-chance process; individualized, job-related review; candidate privacy; lawful licensing analysis; outcome reporting; and a credible advancement pathway. A pledge or logo alone does not establish verified status.

Prohibited conduct

  • Submitting knowingly false, privileged, stolen, or unlawfully obtained material.
  • Charging candidates for platform access or placement.
  • Scraping, bulk downloading, or attempting to identify private participants.
  • Using platform information for harassment, surveillance, blacklisting, or any legally restricted eligibility decision.
  • Publishing exact release destinations, home addresses, medical information, Social Security numbers, or protected communications.

Moderation and removal

Justice Grows may hold, correct, annotate, suspend, or remove records, profiles, employers, jobs, and accounts when evidence is incomplete, consent is missing, safety risk is credible, policy is breached, or law requires action.

Employer accountability

Coalition standing may be downgraded or removed for privacy violations, discrimination, blanket exclusions, exploitative compensation, misrepresentation, non-reporting, retaliation, or failure to honor candidate consent.

Legal review

These launch terms are an operating draft. Counsel must adapt them to the final entity, jurisdictions, services, insurance coverage, data flows, fundraising model, and employment relationships before production launch.
