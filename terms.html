Prohibition Careers is a pilot initiative in formation. These terms define the launch-stage service.

No guaranteed outcome

Participation does not guarantee contact, referral, interview, employment, retention, licensing, record relief, funding, social-equity eligibility, or any other result. Employers make their own decisions.

Information on this site is general and is not legal, tax, employment, licensing, financial, or compliance advice. Laws and rules vary by jurisdiction, role, employer, and individual circumstances.

The launch network focuses on people whose careers are affected by nonviolent cannabis convictions. A conviction does not automatically qualify a person for active introduction.

Employers remain responsible for lawful hiring, background-check, licensing, wage-and-hour, workplace, anti-discrimination, and cannabis compliance.
