How Reserve Standard handles information.

This page describes how Reserve Standard handles information collected through the public site, the Bitcoin Treasury Review form, and direct email inquiries.

Last updated: April 2026Reserve Standard LLCtreasury@reservestandard.com

Information collected

  • Basic site analytics and technical log information, if enabled.
  • Information you submit through the Bitcoin Treasury Review form, such as name, business, email, reserve budget range, and treasury-related context.
  • Communications you send directly by email.

How information is used

  • To review fit, respond to inquiries, and schedule Treasury Reviews.
  • To improve the site, messaging, and client onboarding process.
  • To maintain records related to operations, legal compliance, or business administration.

Sharing and retention

Reserve Standard may use service providers for website hosting, analytics, email, or document storage. Information is not sold. Information may be disclosed when reasonably necessary for legal compliance, business protection, or service delivery. Information is retained only as long as reasonably necessary for inquiry handling, recordkeeping, or legal obligations.

Your choices

You may request updates or deletion of submitted contact information by emailing treasury@reservestandard.com, subject to legal, operational, or recordkeeping requirements.
