How Reserve Standard handles information.

This page describes how Reserve Standard handles information collected through the public site, Reserve Review application, and direct email inquiries.

Last updated: April 2026Contact: hello@your-domain.com

Information collected

  • Basic site analytics and technical log information, if enabled.
  • Information you submit through the Reserve Review application, such as name, business, email, reserve budget range, and reserve-related context.
  • Communications you send directly by email.

How information is used

  • To review fit, respond to inquiries, and schedule Reserve Reviews.
  • To improve the site, messaging, and client onboarding process.
  • To maintain records related to operations, legal compliance, or business administration.

Sharing and service providers

Reserve Standard may use service providers for website hosting, analytics, scheduling, email, or document storage. Information is not sold. Information may be disclosed when reasonably necessary for legal compliance, business protection, or service delivery.

Retention and security

Information is retained only as long as reasonably necessary for inquiry handling, recordkeeping, legal compliance, or service delivery. No method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be perfectly secure, so businesses should avoid sending sensitive credentials, private keys, or unnecessary confidential information.

Your choices

You may request updates or deletion of submitted contact information by emailing hello@your-domain.com, subject to legal, operational, or recordkeeping requirements.

Reserve Standard may update this policy from time to time as the site, tools, or operational workflows change.
